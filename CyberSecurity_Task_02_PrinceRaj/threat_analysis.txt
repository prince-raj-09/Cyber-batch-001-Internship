THREAT ANALYSIS

1. INSIDER THREAT

Description:
A threat originating from employees, students, or authorized users.

Possible Impact:
Data theft, unauthorized access, and information leakage.

Prevention:
Access controls, monitoring, and security awareness training.

---

2. MALWARE

Description:
Malicious software designed to damage or compromise systems.

Possible Impact:
Data loss, service disruption, and unauthorized access.

Prevention:
Antivirus software, updates, and safe browsing practices.

---

3. PHISHING

Description:
Fraudulent emails or messages designed to steal information.

Possible Impact:
Credential theft and account compromise.

Prevention:
User awareness training and MFA.

---

4. WEAK PASSWORDS

Description:
Simple or reused passwords that are easy to guess.

Possible Impact:
Unauthorized account access.

Prevention:
Strong password policies and password managers.

---

5. UNPATCHED SYSTEMS

Description:
Systems that have not received security updates.

Possible Impact:
Exploitation of known vulnerabilities.

Prevention:
Regular patch management and vulnerability assessments.
