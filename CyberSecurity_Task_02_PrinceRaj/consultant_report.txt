CYBERSECURITY CONSULTANT REPORT

Subject: Improving College Cybersecurity Posture

Top 5 Risks

1. Student Data Breaches
2. Phishing Attacks
3. Malware Infections
4. Weak Password Usage
5. Unpatched Systems

Top 5 Recommendations

1. Enable Multi-Factor Authentication
2. Enforce Strong Password Policies
3. Conduct Regular Security Awareness Training
4. Implement Automated Patch Management
5. Maintain Regular Data Backups

Expected Benefits

* Improved protection of student and faculty information.
* Reduced risk of unauthorized access.
* Better resilience against ransomware attacks.
* Faster recovery from security incidents.
* Enhanced trust in college information systems.

Conclusion

By implementing these recommendations, the college can significantly strengthen its cybersecurity posture and reduce the likelihood of successful cyber attacks.
