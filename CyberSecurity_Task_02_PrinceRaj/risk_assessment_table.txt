COLLEGE RISK ASSESSMENT

| Asset                      | Threat                    | Impact | Risk Level |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------ | ---------- |
| Student Records            | Data Breach               | High   | High       |
| Examination Data           | Unauthorized Modification | High   | High       |
| College Website            | DDoS Attack               | Medium | Medium     |
| Faculty Information        | Phishing Attack           | High   | High       |
| Network Infrastructure     | Malware Infection         | High   | High       |
| Email System               | Account Compromise        | Medium | Medium     |
| Learning Management System | Ransomware                | High   | High       |

Summary:

Student records, examination data, and network infrastructure represent the highest-risk assets because compromise could significantly impact academic operations and privacy.
