CYBER ATTACK CASE STUDY – WANNACRY RANSOMWARE

What Happened?

WannaCry was a global ransomware attack that occurred in May 2017. The malware exploited a vulnerability in Microsoft Windows systems and rapidly spread across networks. Once infected, files were encrypted and victims were asked to pay a ransom in Bitcoin to regain access.

Which Part of the CIA Triad Was Affected?

1. Availability – Systems and files became inaccessible after encryption.
2. Integrity – Data was modified and encrypted without authorization.

What Was the Impact?

* More than 200,000 computers were affected worldwide.
* Hospitals, businesses, and government organizations experienced service disruptions.
* Financial losses reached billions of dollars.
* Critical services became unavailable.

How Could It Have Been Prevented?

* Installing security updates promptly.
* Maintaining regular backups.
* Using antivirus and endpoint protection solutions.
* Conducting vulnerability management.
* Educating users about cybersecurity risks.

Conclusion:

The WannaCry attack demonstrated the importance of patch management, backups, and proactive cybersecurity practices.
